About Me

I am currently a research assistant at the Lab for Atomic and Photonic Technologies at Northwestern University, where my primary research is into applications of analog and digital holography, with a particular focus on optical signal processing and data acquisition. I also research the development of photonic integrated circuits in AlGaAs/GaAs and LiNbO3 for fast and slow light gyroscopes using Rb atoms.
I'm passionate about developing new and interesting ways to use the complex nature of light in everything from fundamental science to entertainment.Light is an incredible tool that we are only just beginning to truly understand, but we still have many challenges to overcome if we intend to dominate it as well as we do electronics
